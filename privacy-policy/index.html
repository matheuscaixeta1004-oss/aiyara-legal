Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Aiyara ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our TikTok integration tool used exclusively for managing our virtual content creator account.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate our content publishing tool:

  • TikTok account authentication tokens, used solely to publish video content on behalf of our own account.
  • Basic account information returned by TikTok's Login Kit (such as account ID and username), used only for authentication purposes.

2. How We Use Information

All collected information is used exclusively for:

  • Authenticating with the TikTok API.
  • Uploading and publishing video content to our own TikTok creator account.

We do not sell, share, rent, or otherwise disclose any data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored securely on our private servers and are never shared. No personally identifiable information of any third party is collected or stored.

4. Third-Party Services

Our tool integrates with the TikTok API. Use of TikTok's services is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Authentication tokens are retained only as long as necessary to maintain the connection to TikTok's API. They may be revoked or deleted at any time through TikTok's account settings.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
